# 🏨 SeaShore Hotel Management System

A full-stack **Hospitality Management Platform** built with Next.js, designed to manage hotel operations including room booking, reservations, billing, and administrative workflows.

🌐 Live Platform: https://hotelseashorebd.com/  
💻 Frontend: https://github.com/anisurarzu/HotelSeaShore-Client  
⚙️ Backend: https://github.com/anisurarzu/HotelSeaShore-Server  

---

## 🚀 Overview

SeaShore is a **real-world hotel management system** developed to streamline hospitality operations such as reservations, billing, and resource management.

The platform focuses on **performance, usability, and scalability**, making it suitable for modern hotel business environments.

---

## ✨ Key Features

### 🏨 Hotel Management
- Room booking & reservation system  
- Availability tracking  
- Calendar-based booking system  

### 💳 Billing & Finance
- Invoice generation  
- Payment tracking  
- Expense management  

### 📊 Dashboard & Analytics
- Booking insights  
- Revenue tracking  
- Performance monitoring  

### 📦 Inventory & Operations
- Resource & supply management  
- Operational workflow automation  

### 🔐 Authentication & Roles
- Role-based access control  
- Admin & staff management
